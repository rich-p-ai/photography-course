# Day 10 – Mixed Genre: Portrait in Landscape

**Genre focus:** Integration
**Time:** Blue hour
**Location suggestions:** IOP beach or Charleston waterfront with a person.

## Study Material
- Combine the composition skills from Days 1-3 with portrait technique.
- Subject should feel part of the place, not just placed in front of it.

## Shooting Brief
1. One person + strong landscape elements.
2. Use leading lines or frames to connect them.
3. Balance exposure between person and background.
