# Day 02 – Framing & Layers

**Genre focus:** Street / Architecture
**Time:** Morning blue hour
**Location suggestions:** Doorways, windows, palm trees, arches in Charleston; beach fencing or dunes at IOP.

## Study Material
- Use natural frames (windows, arches, trees) to isolate the subject.
- Create depth with foreground / midground / background layers.

## Shooting Brief
1. Find frames that contain a clear subject.
2. Include at least one layered shot with three distinct planes.
3. Keep the 24-50mm; try both ends of the zoom.

## Upload
Best 3–5 images + reflection.md (same template as Day 01).
