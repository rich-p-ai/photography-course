# Day 05 – Silhouettes & Contrast

**Genre focus:** Landscape / Street
**Time:** Blue hour into early sunrise
**Location suggestions:** IOP pier, Charleston Battery, or any strong shape against the sky.

## Study Material
- Meter for the bright sky so the subject goes black.
- Clean, recognizable shapes work best.

## Shooting Brief
1. Find strong silhouettes (people, trees, architecture, pier).
2. Keep the horizon clean.
3. Experiment with partial silhouettes.
