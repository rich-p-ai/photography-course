# Day 07 – Environmental Portraits (Self / Family)

**Genre focus:** Portrait
**Time:** Blue hour or soft morning light
**Location suggestions:** Home, yard, quiet Charleston street, or IOP dune path.

## Study Material
- Place the person in a meaningful environment.
- Use f/2.8–4 for subject separation if desired.
- Eye AF + continuous.

## Shooting Brief
1. Self-portraits or family/friends.
2. Show the environment – do not crop too tight.
3. Direct soft light onto the face if possible.

## Upload
Best 3–5 + reflection.md
