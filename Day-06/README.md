# Day 06 – Reflections & Water

**Genre focus:** Landscape
**Time:** Blue hour
**Location suggestions:** IOP beach, tidal pools, Charleston harbor, puddles after rain.

## Study Material
- Look for mirror-like surfaces.
- Include both the subject and its reflection, or abstract the reflection alone.

## Shooting Brief
1. Low angle for maximum reflection.
2. Polarizer if you have one (optional).
3. Capture both still and slightly moving water.
